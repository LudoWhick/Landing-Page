Anciens city of Alexandria

Ancient Alexandria, founded by Alexander the Great in 331 BC, was a thriving hub of knowledge, trade, and culture. Home to the renowned Library of Alexandria and the iconic Pharos lighthouse, it symbolized a fusion of Egyptian and Greek influences. Its legacy as a center of learning and cross-cultural exchange remains an enduring fascination.
